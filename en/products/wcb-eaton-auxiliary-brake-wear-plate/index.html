WCB Eaton Auxiliary Brake Wear Plate

WCB Eaton auxiliary brake wear plate for Eaton WCB124, WCB224, WCB324, WCB236, WCB336 and WCB436 auxiliary brake systems. WCB water-cooled brakes are used for continuous slip braking and high-temperature working conditions; send the WCB model, part number or product photo for matching.

Reference Product Information

Auxiliary brake models include Eaton WCB124, WCB224, WCB324, WCB236, WCB336 and WCB436. The WCB water-cooled brake uses pneumatic control and a closed or open cooling circuit with coolant flow for heat transfer.

Wear Plate Function

The wear plate is listed in the reference parameter table as 耐磨板. It supports WCB brake maintenance where continuous slip braking and heat dissipation are required.
