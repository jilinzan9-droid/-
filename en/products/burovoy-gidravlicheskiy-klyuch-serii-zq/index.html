Product Overview

ZQ series drilling hydraulic tongs are designed for drill pipe make-up and break-out. They provide efficient, simple, safe and reliable operation, reduce manual labor intensity, and support accurate thread connection and disconnection during drilling and workover operations.

The compact construction is easy to operate and maintain. Hydraulic backup tong options can be supplied for ZQ127/25, ZQ162/35, ZQ203/100 and ZQ203/125 model requirements.
