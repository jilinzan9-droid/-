Oilfield Equipment Spare Parts Sourcing Support

Jinan Prite Petroleum Equipment supports buyers who need multiple oilfield spare parts categories in one sourcing workflow.

WhatsApp inquiryCompany News

Many procurement teams do not search for one single item only. They need coordinated quotation support across pumps, rigs, wellhead equipment and maintenance parts.

Wide spare parts categories

Pratt Oil can support inquiries for drilling rig spare parts, mud pump spare parts, workover equipment components, wellhead tools, oilfield valves, drawworks brake system parts and hydraulic maintenance components.

Matching by part number, drawing or sample

Oilfield parts often have different names, catalog numbers or modified dimensions. Buyers can send part number, model, drawing, product photo, sample photo and operating conditions so the team can check specification matching before quotation.

Suitable for regular procurement

This sourcing workflow is useful when a buyer needs several fast-moving oilfield spare parts in one inquiry, such as mud pump consumables, rig brake parts, seals, valves, hoses, gauges and wellhead field tools.

Contact CTA

For efficient communication, prepare product names, quantities, destination and available drawings. WhatsApp and email inquiry support are available for overseas buyers.
