High-wear mud pump consumables are frequently replaced in drilling operations. Learn what information buyers should send for fast quotation.

Procurement scenario

Mud pump liners, pistons, valves and seats are fast-wearing oilfield parts used in daily rig maintenance. Drilling sites in desert and offshore operations often replace these drilling consumables during planned service or urgent repair.

Common product words

Buyers commonly ask for mud pump liners, ceramic liners, bi-metal liners, mud pump pistons, valve assemblies, valves and seats, seals, gaskets and related oilfield maintenance parts.

Information buyers should send

Send part number, pump model, liner size, piston size, valve type, seat type, quantity, destination country and product photo. If the site does not have a part number, a clear drawing or used-part photo can support quotation and compatibility checking.

WhatsApp inquiry CTA

For quotation, buyers can send part number, pump model, rig model, product photo, technical drawing, quantity and destination. WhatsApp inquiry is suitable for fast communication when multiple images or drawings need to be checked.
