Procurement scenario

Oilfield equipment spare parts exported from China may require different packing methods according to product type, quantity, weight and shipment plan. Buyers can request quotation support before confirming mud pump parts, brake parts, hydraulic components or field maintenance parts.

Quotation details

Useful inquiry data includes model, part number, technical drawing, product photo, quantity, material requirement and delivery destination. Clear data helps avoid mismatching and supports practical request quote communication.

Export packaging support

Packing communication may include carton, wooden case or pallet arrangements depending on the spare parts. Buyers can use WhatsApp inquiry or email to confirm export packaging notes for Middle East deliveries.
