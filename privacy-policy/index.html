Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Jinan Prite Petroleum Equipment Co., Ltd. uses inquiry information submitted by oilfield equipment spare parts buyers.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to check product compatibility, prepare quotation details, arrange technical communication and support export packaging or shipping discussion.
